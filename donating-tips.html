Choose the Right Items

Only donate items that are accepted by the donation centres.

New or Gently Used Items

Ensure items are new or gently used; suitable for reuse.

Label Fragile Items

If you're donating fragile items, please label them clearly.

Not a Dumping Ground

Donating centres are not dumping grounds. Please arrange the items in order.

Do not donate dangerous items

Chemicals, flammable materials or sharp objects are prohibited.
