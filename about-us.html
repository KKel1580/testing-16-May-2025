Who We Are

We believe that giving should be simple, meaningful, and impactful. "Donate To Who" is a platform dedicated to connecting donors with the right donation centres that need their help the most.

Whether you're giving away books, clothes, toys, or furniture, our mission is to ensure your generosity reaches the right hands.

Thank you for choosing to make a difference.
